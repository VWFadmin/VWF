Interest-Free Loans

Veritas Wealth Fellowship is a social movement which seeks to eliminate usury across the United States. VWF seeks to empower local communities by offering interest-free loans to businesses and individuals. Whether you're a business owner who needs additional funding for a business venture or expansion, or a student who's looking for alternatives to the traditional student-loan system to pay for education, or whether one is looking to build up their credit-score through a small loan amount, VWF offers a number of usury-free loan programs to qualifying individuals

Christian Wealth Builder Program

Our Veritas Wealth Fellowship offers members the opportunity to radically change their communities while growing their wealth. The Christian Wealth Builder Program allows members to donate a specific amount of money to the VWF, over a given time interval. With these generous donations, VWF will invest within the general community using Chrisitan-based wealth management principles. As a benefit of membership, members of the CWBP are then entitled to share the risk/reward of investing within one's community.

Grow your wealth in alignment with Scripture, such as Proverbs 22:7.
